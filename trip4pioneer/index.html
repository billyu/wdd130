Tracing Early Church History

Get ready for an adventure of a lifetime this summer as our family sets off to trace the footsteps of early pioneers from New York to Salt Lake City! Covering 2,457 miles over a span of 14 days, we'll be embarking on a journey that's sure to leave us breathless and inspired. Our trip is going to be unlike any other because we're going to explore the historical sites of early Church history along the way. We're talking about places like Palmyra, Harmony, Fayette, Kirtland, Nauvoo, Winter Quarters, and Salt Lake City, where you can feel the magic of the pioneers come alive. As we prepare for our trip, we've been collecting all the travel information and church history we can get our hands on. And guess what? We want to share it with you! If you're interested in learning more about Church history and planning to travel the pioneer trail, then you won't want to miss out on our collection of materials. So, come join us on this epic journey as we relive the stories, struggles, and triumphs of our early members. Let's experience the joy and challenges together, and create memories that will last a lifetime!
