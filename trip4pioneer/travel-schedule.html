Discover the Heart of America:

Take an Unforgettable Road Trip from New York to Salt Lake City!

Buckle up and get ready to explore the heart of America with us as we drive from New York to Salt Lake City! Our route will take us through ten incredible states: New York, Pennsylvania, Ohio, Indiana, Illinois, Missouri, Iowa, Nebraska, Wyoming, and Utah. With each state offering its unique blend of history, culture, and natural beauty, this trip promises to be a feast for the senses. From the rugged terrain of Wyoming to the rolling hills of Pennsylvania, we'll be treated to stunning landscapes that will take our breath away. Along the way, we'll encounter friendly locals, vibrant communities, and charming towns that are sure to capture our hearts. We'll also have plenty of opportunities to indulge in local cuisine, visit historical sites, and take part in outdoor activities that will keep us energized and engaged. So, if you're up for an epic road trip adventure that will take you through some of America's most iconic landmarks and hidden gems, then join us on this unforgettable journey from New York to Salt Lake City!

Day 1: New York to Harmony to Rochester

We'll start our journey in New York City, where we'll meet up with our fellow travelers and begin our road trip adventure. After a quick stop at the Empire State Building, we'll head out of the city and make our way to Harmony, Pennsylvania, where we'll visit the site of the first Latter-day Saint meetinghouse in the United States. From there, we'll continue on to Rochester, where we'll spend the night.

Day 2: Visit Palmyra and Fayette

After a hearty breakfast, we'll head out of Rochester and make our way to Palmyra, New York, where we'll visit the site of the Smith family farm and the Hill Cumorah. From there, we'll continue on to Fayette, where we'll spend the night.

Day 3: Visit Niagara Falls

After a hearty breakfast, we'll head out of Fayette and make our way to Niagara Falls, where we'll spend the night.

Day 4: Rochester to Cleveland

After a hearty breakfast, we'll head out of Rochester and make our way to Cleveland, where we'll spend the night.

Day 5: Visit Kirtland

Day 6: Kirtland to Nauvoo

Day 7: Nauvoo to Winter Quarters

After a hearty breakfast, we'll head out of Nauvoo and make our way to Winter Quarters, where we'll spend the night.

Day 8: Winter Quarters to Cheyenne

After a hearty breakfast, we'll head out of Winter Quarters and make our way to Cheyenne, where we'll spend the night.

Day 9: Cheyenne to Salt Lake City

After a hearty breakfast, we'll head out of Cheyenne and make our way to Salt Lake City, where we'll spend the night.

Day 10, 11: Visit Salt Lake City and Temple Square

After a hearty breakfast, we'll head out of Salt Lake City and make our way to Temple Square, where we'll spend the night.

Day 12: Visit Yellow Stone

After a hearty breakfast, we'll head out of Salt Lake City and make our way to Yellow Stone, where we'll spend the night.

Day 13: Visit BYU Idaho

After a hearty breakfast, we'll head out of Salt Lake City and make our way to BYU Idaho, where we'll spend the night.

Day 14: Head back to New York

After a hearty breakfast, we'll head out of Salt Lake City and make our way back to New York, where we'll spend the night.
