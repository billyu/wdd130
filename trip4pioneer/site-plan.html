Overview

Purpose

This website is designed to describe the places (and their history) of our family's summer trip to the U.S. This trip is special to us because we will visit many historical sites of early Church history, like Palmyra, Harmony, Nauvoo, and Salt Lake City. I hope this website will motivate others to learn more about Church history and travel the pioneer trail to experience the joy and challenges of our early members.

Audience

The audience will be all interested in traveling and visiting Church historical sites.

Content

Home page

Get ready for an adventure of a lifetime this summer as our family sets off to trace the footsteps of early pioneers from New York to Salt Lake City! Covering 2,457 miles over a span of 14 days, we'll be embarking on a journey that's sure to leave us breathless and inspired. Our trip is going to be unlike any other because we're going to explore the historical sites of early Church history along the way. We're talking about places like Palmyra, Harmony, Fayette, Kirtland, Nauvoo, Winter Quarters, and Salt Lake City, where you can feel the magic of the pioneers come alive. As we prepare for our trip, we've been collecting all the travel information and church history we can get our hands on. And guess what? We want to share it with you! If you're interested in learning more about Church history and planning to travel the pioneer trail, then you won't want to miss out on our collection of materials. So, come join us on this epic journey as we relive the stories, struggles, and triumphs of our early members. Let's experience the joy and challenges together, and create memories that will last a lifetime!

Travel Schedule

Buckle up and get ready to explore the heart of America with us as we drive from New York to Salt Lake City! Our route will take us through ten incredible states: New York, Pennsylvania, Ohio, Indiana, Illinois, Missouri, Iowa, Nebraska, Wyoming, and Utah. With each state offering its unique blend of history, culture, and natural beauty, this trip promises to be a feast for the senses. From the rugged terrain of Wyoming to the rolling hills of Pennsylvania, we'll be treated to stunning landscapes that will take our breath away. Along the way, we'll encounter friendly locals, vibrant communities, and charming towns that are sure to capture our hearts. We'll also have plenty of opportunities to indulge in local cuisine, visit historical sites, and take part in outdoor activities that will keep us energized and engaged. So, if you're up for an epic road trip adventure that will take you through some of America's most iconic landmarks and hidden gems, then join us on this unforgettable journey from New York to Salt Lake City! Historic Sites

Get ready to witness history come alive as we explore the Church's Historic Sites on this trip of a lifetime! One of the most exciting things about this journey is that we'll be visiting places we've only read about in books, and now we get to experience them firsthand and create memories that will last a lifetime. Our itinerary is packed with incredible destinations, each with its unique story to tell. From the Sacred Grove, where Joseph Smith saw the First Vision, to Hill Cumorah, where the Book of Mormon was translated, we'll be walking in the footsteps of our early Church leaders. Other notable sites on our list include the Whitmer Farm, where the Church was officially organized, the Grandin Building, where the first copies of the Book of Mormon were printed, and the Priesthood Restoration Site, where the Aaronic and Melchizedek Priesthoods were restored. We'll also be visiting Joseph Smith's Birthplace, Historic Kirtland, Historic Nauvoo, Carthage Jail, and the Mormon Trail Center at Historic Winter Quarters, where we'll get to learn more about the history and legacy of the Church. And of course, no trip to the Church's Historic Sites would be complete without a visit to the iconic Salt Lake Temple, the spiritual heart of the Church. So come join us on this journey of discovery and inspiration, as we explore the Church's Historic Sites and create memories that will last a lifetime.
